## Use Cases by Actor

### Library Patrons (4 use cases)

1. **Search and Browse Books** — A patron searches the catalog, filters by genre/author/ISBN, and browses available books

2. **Check Out Books** — A patron selects books and completes the checkout process, receiving a digital or physical receipt

3. **Renew Book Loan** — A patron renews an active book loan to extend the due date

4. **Place Book Hold/Reservation** — A patron places a hold on an unavailable book to be notified when it becomes available

### Librarians (3 use cases)

5. **Process Book Returns** — A librarian scans and processes returned books, updating inventory and handling overdue fees

6. **Manage Patron Accounts** — A librarian creates accounts, resets passwords, updates contact information, and handles membership issues

7. **Catalog New Books** — A librarian adds new books to the system, including metadata, location assignment, and inventory tracking

### Library Manager (2 use cases)

8. **Generate Reports and Analytics** — A manager generates reports on circulation, patron statistics, overdue items, and collection performance

9. **Manage Library Inventory** — A manager monitors stock levels, identifies unused books, and makes acquisition decisions based on demand

### Book Supplier (1 use case)

10. **Submit and Track Supply Orders** — A supplier receives book orders, updates order status, and manages delivery schedules
